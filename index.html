Mamshad Nayeem Rizve

I am a fourth year PhD student at Center for Research in Computer Vision, UCF, where I am advised by Prof. Mubarak Shah. I'm interested in computer vision, and machine learning. My research primarily focuses on learning with limited labels, which mostly includes semi-supervised learning, few-shot learning, and self-supervised learning. I have also worked on an IARPA funded research project involving activity detection.
